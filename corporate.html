Programme format

A structured 3-day experience

Arrival and baseline

The retreat begins with orientation and a Yoga Foundations session designed to bring participants to a shared baseline, making the full programme accessible from Day 1 regardless of prior experience.

Progressive journey

Across the retreat, participants move through Yoga, Mindfulness, Pilates, Coaching, reflection circles, and integration in a thoughtfully designed sequence.

Day 0
Arrival, Orientation, Yoga Foundations, Welcome Dinner.
Day 1
Ayurvana Holistic Flow, Grounding Awareness, Group Coaching, Pilates Foundations, Reflection.
Day 2
Powerhouse Pilates, Deepened Presence, Integration Workshop, Temple Purification Ritual, Flow Focus 1.
Day 3
Flow Focus 2, Integration & Presence, Action Planning, Integrated Athletic Pilates Flow, Closing Ceremony.
